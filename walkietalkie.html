Walkie Talkie

Do you have what it takes?

"You assume the role of a psychic government agent and investigate anomalies in a bizarre and warped United States of America. On your adventures you will encounter many strange and dangerous situations In-which you will have to walk, talk and battle your way through in order to defeat the son-of-a-bitch that's causing all the mayhem."

Walkie Talkie is the latest exciting project I am currently working on. It is a first person adventure game where you walk around, talk to people and solve mysteries. I'm working with a small but talented team of a Producer/Designer/Artist and a Musician. We will be getting our game crowdfunded on kickstarter very soon.
