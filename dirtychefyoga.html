Dirty Chef Yoga

Cooking madness with Chef Yoga!

Dirty Chef Yoga was the result of a casual three day game jam. Our team had to make a game out of what was generated using The Video Game Name Generator (www.videogamena.me). And as you can see we were very lucky to get 'Dirty Chef Yoga' :P.

However, we made the most of it and in the end created something that we were quite proud of especially considering the short time we had.

I was responsible for implementing these system:
- Player controller
- Player Input controllers: Xbox controller input, Keyboard input
- Player action controller: interaction between ingredients and stations
- Ingredient and burger stacking system
- Setting up patty cooking shader to work with grill
- Food spawner and conveyor belt
- Implementing stations: deep fryer, grill, bench, trashcan, pass and order submission
- Gizmos and debug systems
- Robot animations: idle, pickup, holding item, panic
- Hooking up audio, animations and particle fxs
- General architecture and software engineering
