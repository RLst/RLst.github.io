About Me

Hello and Welcome! My name is Tony, but you can also call me Huy. I'm a passionate and dedicated game developer with over 5 years of experience using Unity. Throughout my career, I have primarily worked remotely, but also gained valuable on-site experience for a month. Programming and game development are not just jobs for me; they are my driving force, and I approach them with enthusiasm, drive, and unwavering commitment.

I hold an Advanced Diploma of Professional Game Development, specializing in Game Programming, from the esteemed Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AE) in Melbourne, Australia. This comprehensive education has equipped me with a strong foundation in game development principles and practices. I strive to write efficient, clean, readable, and extendable code while minimizing the impact on system resources.

In my spare time, I indulge in designing and developing my own games and coding projects. This self-led exploration allows me to continuously challenge myself, sharpen my skills, and enhance my confidence as a developer.

Throughout my journey, I have developed a diverse range of applications, from simple to complex, both as part of my time at AIE, at work and through personal projects. These experiences have honed my problem-solving abilities and enriched my understanding of various aspects of game development.

As an seasoned game developer, I am actively seeking new opportunities in the industry to contribute to programming and game development goals. With my base in HCMC, Vietnam, I am eager to take on fresh challenges and make a positive impact in the field.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with you and exploring potential collaborations in game development and programming.
