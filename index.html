About Me

Hello and welcome! I'm Tony Le and I am a game programming graduate of an Advanced Diploma of Professional Game Development at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment (AIE) in Melbourne and specialising in Game Programming.

Programming and game development is something I do with enthusiasm, drive and commitment. I believe that it is crucial for code to be efficient, clean, readable and extendable, whilst also minimising the impact on system resources.

I have written a number of apps, from simple to complex, as both part of my time at AIE and with my personal projects. My proudest achievements are my contributions to the StormRend project, BhaVE, my Behaviour Tree Visual Editor for Unity and the 3 day Game Jam project Dirty Chef Yoga. I hope you will enjoy browsing through my work below.

During my spare time, I enjoy designing and developing my own games and coding projects. I find that this self-led study and personal development allows me to challenge myself and further hone my skills and confidence.

Being a graduate that has successfully completed a complicated and demanding final major project, believe I am more than prepared to enter the workforce and I am currently and actively seeking work in the industry. I look forward to helping your team meet its programming and game dev goals. 

In the mean time during the pandemic I have been working on a revenue share remote project, so my programming have not stagnated and is still improving each day.

As of right now, I am currently based in Brisbane. Feel free to contact me about my work!
